#17

Description

Philly Cheesesteak with onions and peppers

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Steak ribeye 5 oz
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut 7.5 inches of bread
  2. Lay out 3 slices of american cheese
  3. Cook steak to no pink
  4. 2 oz of onions on grill
  5. 1.5 oz of peppers on grill
  6. Chop 16 times
  7. Melt cheese on top
  8. Insert in bread
