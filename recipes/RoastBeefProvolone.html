#7 Mike's Way

Description

Roastbeef and provolone topped with Mike's Way

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Provolone
  • Roastbeef
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut 7.5 inches of bread
  2. Slice 3 slices of provolone
  3. Slice 5.5 oz of roastbeef
  4. Top with 5 rings of onions
  5. Top with 1 inch of lettuce
  6. Top with 3 slices of tomatoes
  7. Juice 1 oz of vinegar
  8. Juice with 1 oz of oil
  9. Sprinkle oregano on top
  10. Sprinkle salt on top
